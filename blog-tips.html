    5 Tips for the Perfect Custom Portrait

    ← Back to blog

    June 10, 2026

    1. Pick the Right Photo

    A clear, well-lit face shot. Natural light. A smile or mid-laugh gives way more personality than a stiff pose.

    2. Tell Us Who They Are

    The football star. The cheerleader. The gym rat. The giggle boss. Tell us their sport, their obsession, their funny sayings.

    3. Include Their Favorites

    A basketball. A cheer bow. A favorite stuffed animal. These details make the portrait uniquely theirs.

    4. Think About Where It's Going

    Bedroom poster? Living room frame? Knowing where it lives helps us choose the right style.

    5. Trust the Process

    Three concepts to choose from. Unlimited revisions. Satisfaction guaranteed.
