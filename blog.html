What Happens When You Order a Custom Portrait

Send us a photo, tell us who your kid really is, and we get to work. Within a few days, you'll receive three different concepts to choose from. We'll revise until it's perfect or refund you fully.

June 9, 2026

5 Tips for Choosing the Perfect Kid Portrait

1. Pick their best laugh — the real smile, not the posed one.
2. Include what they love — a ball, a dance pose, their favourite toy.
3. Think about where it'll go — bedroom wall, tee, or frame?
4. Don't stress the pose — we'll illustrate them naturally.
5. Trust the process — three concepts, unlimited revisions.
