Welcome to OTK Design Studio — A Family-Run Shop

We're an American, Jamaican, Peruvian, and German family raising three kids who live on courts, fields, and mats. OTK started when we made personality posters for our own kids — capturing their sports, their obsessions, their funny little sayings. Turns out every family wants to see what makes theirs special, drawn the way they really are.

Everything we make is designed in-house by our family. From oversized tees to custom illustrated portraits, every piece starts with a real moment — a stage, a personality, an era worth keeping.

We're so glad you're here. Browse the shop, read our story, and if you love something, know it was made with heart.

What Happens When You Order a Custom Portrait

You send us a photo, tell us who your kid really is — the loud one, the gym rat, the giggle boss — and we get to work. Within a few days, you'll receive three different illustrated concepts to choose from.

Once you pick your favourite, we finalize the design and send you the file. You can print it as a poster, frame it, or have it printed on a tee or mug. And if you don't absolutely love it, we'll revise it until you do — or refund you fully.

It's that simple. Every order is drawn, checked, and approved by our family.

June 9, 2026

5 Tips for Choosing the Perfect Kid Portrait

1. Pick their best laugh. The photo that captures their real smile — not the posed one — will make the best portrait.

2. Include their thing. Whether it's a soccer ball, a dance pose, or their favourite stuffed animal, including what they love makes the portrait truly them.

3. Think about where it'll go. A poster for their bedroom wall? A tee for game day? A framed print for the living room? Knowing where it lives helps us design the right format.

4. Don't stress the pose. We'll illustrate your child in a way that feels natural — sitting, standing, mid-laugh, mid-dribble. You just provide the inspiration.

5. Trust the process. You'll see three concepts before we settle on the final design. If you want changes, we make them. No pressure, no rush.
