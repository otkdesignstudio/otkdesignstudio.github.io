Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

OTK Design Studio ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website otkdesignstudio.com and our Etsy shop at etsy.com/shop/OTKDesignStudio. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our services, including our Pinterest integration.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact information you provide when placing a custom order (name, email via Etsy messaging)
  • Photos and personal descriptions you submit for custom portrait or personalised poster orders
  • Order details including product selection, shipping address (handled by Etsy and our print providers)
  • Pinterest account information when you interact with our pins or boards

How We Use Your Information

  • To fulfil your custom orders — portraits, kid posters, and personalised products
  • To communicate with you about your order via Etsy messaging
  • To create and publish product pins on our Pinterest account (pinterest.com/otkdesignstudio)
  • To improve our products and services
  • To send you order updates and review requests through Etsy's platform

Pinterest Integration

We use the Pinterest API to automatically pin our products to our official Pinterest account (pinterest.com/otkdesignstudio). This integration:

  • Only posts product images and descriptions from our own Etsy listings
  • Does not collect or store any Pinterest user data from visitors
  • Does not access your personal Pinterest account or boards
  • Links directly to our Etsy shop for all purchases
  • Pins include product images, titles, descriptions, and Etsy listing URLs only

Our Pinterest app is used solely for publishing our own product content to our own Pinterest business account. We do not use it to collect, store, or process any data from Pinterest users who view or interact with our pins.

Custom Order Photos

When you submit a photo for a custom portrait or personalised poster order:

  • Your photo is used exclusively to create your specific order
  • We do not share your photo with any third party except our fulfilment providers (Printify, Gelato) as necessary to print your order
  • We do not use your photo for marketing, training AI models, or any other purpose
  • Photos are deleted after your order is completed and delivered

Third Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate our business:

  • Etsy — our primary sales platform. Etsy's privacy policy governs all transactions: etsy.com/legal/privacy
  • Printify — print-on-demand fulfilment for apparel and drinkware
  • Gelato — print-on-demand fulfilment for prints, cards, and framed products
  • Pinterest — social media platform for product discovery

Each of these services has their own privacy policy governing how they handle data.

Data Storage and Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously. Custom order details and photos are handled securely and are only accessible to our small family team. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Children's Privacy

Many of our products are designed for children. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13. Custom orders involving children's portraits are submitted by parents or guardians, and we treat all such information with the utmost care and confidentiality.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Request that we correct inaccurate information
  • Withdraw consent for us to use your photos at any time before your order is fulfilled

Cookies

Our website itself does not use cookies. Our third-party platforms (Etsy, Pinterest) may use cookies according to their own privacy policies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
